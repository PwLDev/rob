Rob Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-06-18

Introduction

Rob ("the Bot") is a Discord bot designed to interact with users and provide AI-generated responses. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Bot processes, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Process

The Bot may process the following information:

Discord Information

  • User IDs
  • Usernames and display names
  • Server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Message content sent in channels the Bot has read access to
  • Direct messages sent to the Bot

Server Configuration Data

The Bot stores server-specific settings, including:

  • Bot configuration preferences
  • Trusted server addresses used by the mail feature
  • Designated mail channels

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Generate AI-powered responses
  • Maintain short-term conversation context
  • Operate bot features and commands
  • Deliver inter-server mail messages
  • Manage server-specific settings
  • Improve reliability and functionality

AI Processing

Messages that require AI-generated responses may be transmitted to Groqcloud, our sole third-party AI service provider, for processing.

This provider receives only the information necessary to generate a response, such as message history and usernames. Their handling of data is subject to their own privacy policy and terms of service.

Information on Model Training

As of the latest update of this Policy, Groqcloud does not seem to have any interest in collecting user data for the purposes of training AI models. For the reader of this Privacy Policy's convenience, Groqcloud has stated the following on Article 4.2 of their Services Agreement:

[...] For clarity, Groq is not permitted to use Inputs or Outputs for training or fine-tuning any AI Model Services or other models, unless explicitly granted permission or instructed by Customer.

Data Storage

Temporary Conversation Context

To maintain coherent conversations, the Bot temporarily stores a limited number of recent messages in memory. This information is automatically discarded as older messages are replaced, and is never logged to a persistent storage medium.

Configuration Data

Server configuration settings are stored until:

  • The Bot is removed from the server, or
  • An administrator resets or changes the settings.

Inter-Server Mail Feature

When using the mail feature, message contents and sender names are delivered to the destination server selected by the user. Users should not send sensitive or confidential information through this feature.

Data Sharing

The Bot does not sell, rent, or otherwise monetize user data.

Information may be shared only:

  • With the AI service provider necessary to generate responses
  • When required by law
  • To protect the security or integrity of the Bot and its users

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored configuration data and operational information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for individuals who are prohibited from using Discord under Discord's Terms of Service.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
